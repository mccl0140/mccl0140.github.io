ABOUT ME!

Designer • Illustrator • Animal Lover • Food Enthusiast

Hey there!

My name is Samantha McClelland, but my friends call me Sam. I’m currently an enthusiastic student at Algonquin College studying Graphic Design – Learning the ins and outs of design and illustration. Born and raised in the capital of Canada (Ottawa!) and loving it, although, I do enjoy a good trip! I am a real people person – no seriously, I truly am – I try my hardest to meet new people nearby and abroad during my travels. Smiling, laughing, and networking with people I have just met or chatting it up an old buddy, are just as important to me as design. If I am going to be honest with myself here… I am also a hopeless animal lover. I have two dogs and several small animals including my Chinchilla – Ruu!

Well, that is enough about me!
